## Rules for This Space

- **No product loop.** No PM tickets, no QA gates, no release agent.

- **No PII rules don't apply here** — this is for one person and she knows what she's doing.
- **Experiments can be abandoned without ceremony.**
- **Read before you write.** Still non-negotiable.
