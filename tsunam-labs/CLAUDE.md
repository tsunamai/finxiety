## Rules for This Space

- **No product loop.** No PM tickets, no QA gates, no release agent sign-off required.
- **Phone-first preferred** — but not enforced. These are experiments, not products.
- **No PII stored.** Keep experiments stateless. All logic client-side.
- **Accessibility:** best-effort. WCAG 2.1 AA is not mandatory here.
- **Experiments can be abandoned without ceremony.** Move them to Dormant in the backlog and move on.
- **Read before you write.** Still non-negotiable.
